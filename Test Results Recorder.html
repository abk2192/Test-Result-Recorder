help How to Use the Test Recorder

      

        
    
          
  • Drag & Drop: Grab the drag_indicator handle to reorder scenarios, or drag the tab pills to rearrange tabs.
    • 
          
  • Cloning & Moving: Use the file_copy button to duplicate a scenario, or the move_item button to move it to another tab.
    • 
          
  • Templates: Click save to save a scenario as a reusable template. Access them via the "Templates" button.
    • 
          
  • Compare Mode: Click "Compare" to evaluate "Before" and "After" evidence side-by-side with synchronized scrolling.
    •
